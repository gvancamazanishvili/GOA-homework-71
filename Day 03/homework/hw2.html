Italain Tiramisu

ingredients:

1) Espresso

2) Dark rum

3) Mascarpone

4) Zabaglione

5) Sugar

6) Cream

7) Vanilla extract

8) Ladyfingers

9) Cocoa powder

1) Make zabaglione: Whisk egg yolks with sugar and dark rum over low heat until thick. Let it cool. 2)Mix cream base: Blend mascarpone, vanilla extract, and the cooled zabaglione. 3)Fold in whipped cream gently to make the mixture light and fluffy. 4)oak ladyfingers briefly in espresso mixed with a bit of dark rum. 5)Layer in dish: Alternate soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream mixture. 6)Repeat layers and finish with cream on top. 7)Dust with cocoa powder. 8)Chill for 4+ hours before serving.
